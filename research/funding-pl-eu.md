## 0. Structural findings before any instrument

1. **Almost nothing accepts unregistered applicants.** Pre-incorporation doors:
   PARP Platformy Startowe incubation (§1.1), PARP Laboratorium Innowatora,
   EUDIS defence hackathons, EIT Jumpstarter (2027). Everything else needs a
   registered entity; BGK's dual-use loan additionally requires **12 months of
   registration history** (§2.4). Incorporate early. (FACT)
2. **Voivodeship choice is a funding decision.** Polska Wschodnia (lubelskie,
   podkarpackie, podlaskie, świętokrzyskie, warmińsko-mazurskie, plus
   mazowieckie excluding Warsaw city and its ring powiaty) unlocks FEPW
   instruments — including the only pre-incorporation incubation path — and
   higher aid intensities. BGK premiums are **0% in the Warsaw-capital
   subregion** vs 25–70% elsewhere. (FACT)
3. **EIT Manufacturing is dead** — judicial liquidation since March 2026
   (OLAF-investigated); no 2026 calls, site down. Do not plan around it. (FACT)
4. **EIC Accelerator opened to defence/dual-use on 2026-06-17** — one
   instrument now covers both the civilian platform and the drone/C-UAS line.
   (FACT)

## 3. EU — civilian

### 3.1 EIC Accelerator — primary EU target

- Single startups/SMEs; natural persons intending to set up an SME may start
  the short application but the entity must exist before full
  application/contract. TRL 6–8 — live FDM platform = TRL 6 argument; the
  SLS/MJF/CNC expansion is the project. (FACT)
- Money: grant **<€2.5M** (lump sum) and/or equity **€1–10M** (blended /
  grant-only / investment-only). 2026 budget: Open €414M + Challenges €220M.
  Now open to defence/dual-use (§0.4). (FACT)
- Deadlines: short proposals rolling (monthly batches, first Tuesday 17:00
  Brussels); full-proposal cut-offs remaining 2026: **2026-09-02** and
  **2026-11-04**. Seal of Excellence (13/15) unlocks PL national co-funding.
  Success ~2–5% (ESTIMATE).
- Fits: (a)+(b) at scale. Realistic path: incorporate → short proposal Aug–Sep
  2026 → full 2026-11-04 (or 2027).
- https://eic.ec.europa.eu/eic-funding-opportunities/eic-accelerator_en

### 3.2 EIC Pre-Accelerator — perfect profile fit, wrong year

- Mono-beneficiary SME **in a widening country (Poland eligible)**, TRL ≥4.
  Lump-sum grant at **70% funding rate**; 2027 call: **€500K–1M** (budget
  €40M). Opens **2027-05-05**, deadline **2027-11-18**. Includes Fast Track to
  EIC Accelerator. (FACT)
- Fits: (a) for maturing the SLS/MJF/CNC tech. Decide: wait for May 2027 vs.
  straight to Accelerator short proposal in 2026.
- https://eic.ec.europa.eu/eic-funding-opportunities/eic-pre-accelerator_en

### 3.3 Eurostars (Eureka) — strongest near-term grant odds

- SME-led consortium, ≥2 entities from ≥2 Eurostars countries, SME ≥50% of
  budget, ≤36 months, civil only. Poland participates via NCBR: micro/small up
  to **80% industrial research / 70% experimental development**; medium
  80%/60%. (FACT, Call 11 country sheet)
- **Call 11: deadline 2026-09-10 14:00 CEST** (opened 2026-07-09); Call 12:
  2027-03-04. Typical projects €0.5–1.5M total (ESTIMATE).
- Fits: (a) with a German/Dutch/Nordic partner — tight timeline for Call 11
  (needs a real partner + incorporated SME), otherwise Call 12.
- https://www.eurekanetwork.org/programmes-and-calls/eurostars/eurostars-call-for-projects-september-2026/

### 3.4 Misfits (verified, skip)

- **EIC Transition** — requires results of a prior EU-funded project. (FACT)
- **EIC Pathfinder** — TRL 1–4 research consortia; not for a commercial
  platform. (FACT)
- **Horizon Europe Cluster 4** — no SME mono-beneficiary instrument; join only
  as junior consortium partner (WP 2026–27 adopted 2025-12-11; info days
  2026-10-14/15). (FACT)
- **DIGITAL Europe** — deployment consortia, not SME R&D; use Polish EDIH
  "test-before-invest" services opportunistically. (FACT)
- **EIT Manufacturing** — in judicial liquidation since 2026-03; **EIT
  Jumpstarter** 2026 closed (manufacturing category cancelled); 2027 edition
  possible, prize-scale only. (FACT)
- **InvestEU/EIF** — no direct applications; reaches you via BGK guarantees
  (§2.5) and EIF-backed VCs. (FACT)

## 4. EU / NATO — defence & dual-use

### 4.1 EUDIS (EU Defence Innovation Scheme) — the entry ramp, start now

- DG DEFIS instrument inside EDF explicitly to lower SME/startup barriers;
  €231M in EDF WP 2026. (FACT)
- **Defence hackathons** — individuals/teams, **no company needed**; Spring
  2026 edition ran; Autumn 2026 host/participant calls pending. → accessible
  pre-incorporation, now.
- **Business Accelerator** — 8-month program, **€120k non-dilutive voucher**
  (€50k for top 3), no equity/IPR taken; 2 cohorts/yr; Autumn 2026 cohort
  applications (May–June 2026) likely just closed → target a 2027 cohort once
  incorporated. (FACT)
- **Cascade funding** — small grants via EDF projects, e.g. MaJoR call (from
  2026-01-28) at **€60k/grant**. (FACT)
- https://eudis.europa.eu/index_en

### 4.2 NATO DIANA — ideal structural fit at ~12 months

- Dual-use is a hard requirement; TRL ~3–6 deep tech; incorporated company in
  a NATO state; no clearance to apply. Phase 1: **€100k non-dilutive** +
  6-month accelerator; Phase 2: up to **€300k** more. (FACT)
- Poland presence: **FORT KRAKÓW — DIANA Accelerator Poland** (AGH + Kraków
  Technology Park, opened 2026-01-22) + 7 Polish DIANA test centres. (FACT)
- Timeline: 2026-cohort call **closed 2026-07-03** (missed by 17 days);
  DIANA FAQ hints at a 2026-08-09 second wave (ESTIMATE, diana.nato.int is
  bot-blocked — verify in a browser); next certain window ~June 2027.
- https://www.diana.nato.int · https://diana.krakow.pl

### 4.3 EDF (European Defence Fund) — 12+ months, consortium-gated

- WP 2026: €1bn, 31 topics, **all calls close 2026-09-29 17:00**. Consortium:
  min. 3 entities from 3 Member States (2 for disruptive-tech). Non-thematic
  SME calls: **EDF-2026-LS-DA-SME-NT** (€30M, up to €6M/project) and
  **EDF-2026-LS-DIS-RA-SMERO-NT** (€35M, TRL ≥4, up to €4M) — SME research
  funded up to 100%; "spin-in" of civilian tech explicitly welcomed. (FACT)
- 2026 deadline is unrealistic pre-incorporation; target EDF 2027 with a
  3-country consortium.
- https://defence-industry-space.ec.europa.eu/eu-defence-industry/european-defence-fund-edf-official-webpage-european-commission_en

### 4.4 EIC STEP Scale Up Defence — landmark, but later-stage

- `HORIZON-EIC-2026-DEFENCE-01`, opened 2026-07-01, deadline **2026-10-28**.
  First-ever EU direct equity in defence: **€10–30M** per company (2026 budget
  €100M), must catalyse rounds of €50–150M+ with a pre-committed lead investor
  covering ≥20% of the round. (FACT)
- Verdict: growth equity, realistic 2028+ at the earliest. Noted because it
  anchors the dual-use ladder's top rung.
- https://eic.ec.europa.eu/eic-funding-opportunities/eic-step-scale-defence_en

### 4.5 EDIP — capacity-ramp grants, 2027+

- Industrial Reinforcement Actions: lump-sum grants up to 35–50%, max
  €20–30M/project. 2nd Call deadline **2027-02-16** — "Platforms and
  End-products" topic (€152.75M) explicitly covers **Manufacturing as a
  Service** and dual-use heavy-industry conversion — a direct business-model
  match once production capacity exists. USI-IRA (Ukraine-linked): unmanned &
  C-UAS call, same deadline, up to 100% rate, max €10M. (FACT)
- Defence Equity Facility 2.0 (FAST) invests via funds only. (FACT)
- https://defence-industry-space.ec.europa.eu/eu-defence-industry/edip-forging-europes-defence_en

### 4.6 SAFE — not funding, a demand signal

- Up to €150bn in EU-backed loans **to Member States only**. Poland is the
  largest beneficiary (€43.7bn; first €6.56bn disbursed 2026-05-29); covers
  small drones + C-UAS categories. SME access is purely downstream: Polish
  SAFE-financed procurement creates demand for domestic suppliers from 2027.
  (FACT)

### 4.7 PGZ Start-up – Innowacje dla bezpieczeństwa — engage on incorporation

- PGZ + WAT cooperation (signed 2026-06-17); dual-use tech scouting pipeline
  into PGZ's R&D/supplier ecosystem; **Poland-registered entities only**; ~60
  companies in the first edition. Not a published grant scheme — a contact/
  scouting channel. (FACT via Defence24/wojsko-polskie.pl)

### 4.8 NATO Innovation Fund — VC, 12+ months

- €1bn+ fund backed by 24 allies incl. Poland (regional office). First checks
  Seed–Series B, leads up to €15M. Verticals include **Novel Materials and
  Manufacturing**, Autonomy. Relevant once hardware/deep-tech IP exists — not
  for the pure quoting platform. (FACT)
- https://www.nif.fund
